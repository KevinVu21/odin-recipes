Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Egg fried rice is a delicious and popular Asian dish made by stir-frying pre-cooked rice with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and savory seasonings, resulting in a flavorful and satisfying one-pan meal. The combination of fluffy grains of rice, tender eggs, and a medley of vegetables creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes in this classic and versatile dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saut√© onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  3. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
